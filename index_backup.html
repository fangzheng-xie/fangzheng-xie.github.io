About Me

    I am a Ph.D. candidate from the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University. Before I joined the Ph.D. program at JHU, I received my master degree in Applied Mathematics and Statistics at JHU. My research interests include asymptotic theory for nonparametric Bayes, Bayesian nonparametric modeling, Bayesian computation methods, and Bayesian methods for computational biology. My advisor/mentor is Dr. Yanxun Xu.
